Make your AR page

Upload one picture. That's the image people scan with their phone — and it's also what pops up. Everything is compiled right here in your browser.

1. Upload a picture with clear detail (a photo, poster, logo — avoid plain solid colors)
2. Tap Generate — this takes a few seconds
3. A file downloads: ar-page.html
4. Upload it to a free host (GitHub Pages, Netlify Drop) and open the link on a phone
